Formation of OSSSC

As provided in Rule-3 of the Odisha Sub-ordinate Staff Selection (District Cadre) Rules, 2012, the Commission comprises of the Chairman and two Members. The Chairman and the Members of the Commission are appointed by the Government of Odisha and their services are governed in accordance with the provisions of the Odisha Sub-ordinate Staff Selection Commission (Method of Recruitment and Conditions of Service of the Chairman and Members) Rules, 2012.
